The Cataract of Lodore

..The cataract is strong

Then plunges along

Striking and raging

As if a new waging its caverns and rocks among:

Rising and leaping, Sinking and creeping.

Selling and sweeping, Showering and springing

As if a new waging its caverns and rocks among:

Rising and leaping, Sinking and creeping.

Selling and sweeping, Showering and springing

Around and around With endless rebound:

Smiting and fighting. A sight to delight in;

Confounding, astounding, Dizzying and deafening

The car with its sound
